The Best Movies According to Chaz

My Top 3 Movies of all time

Fast and furious

Fast & Furious is an action-packed film series about street racing, heists, and family. It follows Dominic Toretto and his crew as they evolve from underground racers to elite operatives taking on global threats—all while emphasizing loyalty, fast cars, and explosive stunts.

Top Gun: Maverick

Decades after his legendary flying days, Captain Pete "Maverick" Mitchell returns to train a new generation of fighter pilots. As they face a dangerous mission, Maverick must confront his past, prove he still has what it takes, and lead his students to victory.

Jobless Reincarnation

A down-and-out man dies and is reborn in a magical world as Rudeus Greyrat. With his memories intact, he vows to live without regrets—learning magic, forming bonds, and growing stronger as he faces both personal struggles and epic adventures in a fantasy world.
